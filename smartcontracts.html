What is a Smart Contract?

A smart contract is a self-executing agreement written in code, deployed on a blockchain. The contract automatically enforces the terms when conditions are met, without the need for intermediaries. It’s like a digital agreement that runs automatically on Ethereum or Polygon blockchain networks.

How Does It Work?

  • Create the Agreement: Define the conditions (e.g., when to release a payment, transfer tokens, etc.)
  • Deploy to Blockchain: We deploy the contract onto the Ethereum or Polygon blockchain for automation.
  • Automatic Execution: Once the conditions are met, the contract executes without any further input, ensuring fairness and accuracy.

Contract Types and Pricing

Choose the level of complexity you need for your smart contract:

  • Basic Contract ($50): Ideal for straightforward tasks like:

  • Complex Contract ($300): Advanced contracts that require multiple parties, external data, or workflows, such as:
    • Escrow contracts where funds are held in trust until multiple conditions are met.

    • Multifaceted workflows, such as contracts that require approvals from multiple parties before executing actions.
